💡 Tips for Faculty Substitution
General Substitution

Use when a faculty member is absent for an entire day or multiple days. Leave both day and period empty to substitute all their classes.

Partial Substitution

Use when a faculty member is absent only for specific periods. Select the day and period to limit substitutions to that time slot.

Substitution Priority

The system prioritizes replacements by: 1. Teachers with lowest load 2. Teachers who already teach same subject 3. Minimal schedule disruption

After Substitution

A new draft version is created. You can:

  • View the new timetable
  • Make additional adjustments
  • Revert by viewing the original version
